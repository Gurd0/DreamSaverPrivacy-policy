Privacy Policy

DreamSaver  ·  Last updated: March 11, 2026

DreamSaver does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything the app accesses stays entirely on your device.

1. Overview

DreamSaver is an Android screensaver (DreamService) that displays a customisable clock, media player info, battery status, calendar, and a personal photo/video gallery while your device is charging. The app has no internet permission and makes no network connections of any kind.

2. Data We Access – and Why

The app reads certain on-device data solely to render the screensaver display. None of this information leaves your device.

Data / Permission Purpose Stored?
Current time & date Displayed by the clock widget Never
Media session info
(track title, artist, album art) 		Read via Android's MediaSessionManager and shown in the media widget. Requires the Notification Listener permission — however, the app's NotificationListenerService reads no notification content; it is only registered to satisfy Android's API requirement for accessing media sessions. Never
Battery level & charge status Displayed by the battery widget via a system broadcast Never
Photos & videos
(user-selected gallery files) 		Files you explicitly pick in the configuration screen are displayed as screensaver backgrounds. Only the URI references you choose are saved in the app's local SharedPreferences. Local only
System settings
(screensaver_component, enabled_notification_listeners) 		Read-only check during onboarding to show whether setup is complete Never
Boot & power-connected events
(BOOT_COMPLETED, ACTION_POWER_CONNECTED) 		Used to prompt first-time setup (opens the system Dream Settings screen) if the app has not yet been configured as the default screensaver Never

3. App Permissions Explained

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — required by the Android system for services that run while the screen is on.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — allows the app to show a first-time setup reminder after a device restart.
  • WAKE_LOCK — keeps the screen on during the screensaver session.
  • MEDIA_CONTENT_CONTROL / Notification Listener — required by Android to read which media session is currently active. No notification content is read or stored.

4. No Data Collection or Sharing

DreamSaver does not include any analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or third-party SDKs. No data is shared with us or any third party. We have no servers, accounts, or sign-in flows.

5. Local Storage

The only persistent storage the app uses is Android's SharedPreferences, stored locally on your device. It holds your configuration choices (widget layout, colour theme, font, and the URIs of any gallery files you selected). This data never leaves your device and is deleted automatically when you uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

DreamSaver does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all age groups.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository or contact the developer directly through Google Play.
